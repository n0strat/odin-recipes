Loaded Mashed Potatoes

These potatoes are going to change your world. My in-laws ask me to make them for them all the time! They are delicious according to the majority of people who eat them!

Bacon, cheese, and potatoes. How can you go wrong? Try the recipe out, it's easy enough.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Cheddar jack cheese
  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Make a decision on whether or not to peel the potatoes, I personally love potato skin bits inside of my potatoes, but if you don't, feel free to peel them
  2. Boil water to prepare to cook the potatoes
  3. Chop potatoes into quarter inch pieces
  4. Drop chopped potatoes into boiling water, let cook for approximately 25 minutes
  5. While waiting you can cook the bacon
  6. Chop the bacon into small pieces
  7. Remove the potatoes from heat, and drain the water
  8. Put the potatoes in a large mixing bowl, then mash with a mixer or a fork.
  9. Put the bacon, cheese, and sour cream into the potatoes and stir
  10. Season to taste
  11. Serve
