Lazy Pasta Sauce

Description

This pasta sauce is made utilizing jarred sauce rather than homemade tomato sauce.

That being said, it is a super easy meal to make. Here's what you'll need.

Ingredients

  • Your own preferred base sauce
  • Assorted peppers
  • Onions
  • Your choice of ground sausage or beef (I do prefer sausage though)
  • Shredded colby jack cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Chop all of the vegetables into uniform pieces
  2. Saute the vegetables in butter, and salt them as they cook. You only want to partially cook the vegetables as they will continue to cook inside of the sauce
  3. Brown your meat of choice in a separate pan
  4. Bring pasta sauce to a boil
  5. Transfer browned meat and sauteed vegetables into the boiling pasta sauce, stir them in
  6. Allow to cook on a medium-high heat for approximately 20 minutes or until meat is cooked thoroughly, and the vegetables have softened
  7. Remove sauce from heat, stir in shredded cheese and seasonings to taste
  8. Serve and enjoy!
