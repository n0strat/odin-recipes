Crock Pot Queso Dip

Description

This queso dip is super easy to make and it's also in a crock pot! You'll have a delicious dip in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans evaporated milk
  • 1 large jalapeno pepper
  • 1 pound White American cheese
  • 1 pound Pepper Jack cheese

Steps

  1. In the crockpot, take the two cheeses and tear them into small pieces. Layer the cheeses until you run out.
  2. Add evaporated milk
  3. Add diced jalapeno peppers
  4. Stir all ingredients together to make the pot uniform
  5. Put lid on crock pot, use low heat until the mixture is melted (usually right around 2 hours)
  6. Stir and enjoy!
